# Outcome Learning

Prior signal stats are informational only.

Use them to:

- Notice repeated weak setup classes.
- Explain why confidence is lower or why a candidate is skipped.
- Suggest future review topics in `data_gaps` or rationale.

Do not use them to:

- Change fixed signal weights.
- Reduce position sizing.
- Refuse a strong current setup solely because a signal recently lost.
- Promote a weak setup because one signal recently won.

Forward live-paper outcomes are the only valid performance evidence.
